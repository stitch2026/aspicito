Aluminium Extrusions

Aspicito Extrusions

A growing brand in India for customised extrusions manufactured from high-quality billets in a wide range of alloys. We believe in excellent quality, timely delivery, and after-sales service with customer satisfaction.

Our Highlights

Modern technology and a production facility with 5", 6" & 7" interchangeable extrusion presses.

Foundry with modern melting furnaces and the latest HOT TOP casting technology, with a capacity of 24,000 tonnes p.a.

Die design & tool shop with the newest equipment — CNC wire cut, EDM, computerised jig boring, CNC milling and precision measuring with CAD-CAM facilities.

PLC-controlled temperature & timer with 3 anodising bath tanks and 1 electro-colouring tank for up to 25 microns of coating, plus wood & die colouring and sand blasting.

In-house powder coating facilities with high-class techniques and installed capacity for 3,600 tonnes p.a.

Tailored fabrication for aluminium extrusions with drilling, punching and cutting.

Proper quality checks and quality assurance at different stages of manufacturing.

Stretch wrapping with no human touch and customised packing with the desired material.
