Brand Strategy Visual Identity Premium
🖤
Obsidian over Purple
Near-black with a deep violet undertone. Feels more premium than mid-tone purple — it says luxury studio, not tech startup. Infinite contrast for typography.
🔥
Sienna stays, but warmer
Your orange already works well. This refines it slightly — more amber warmth, less harsh. Still energetic and distinctive, but feels like burnished leather rather than traffic cone.
✨
Amber as the surprise
A gold accent that earns its place. Use it sparingly — awards, premium tags, key stats. Signals quality and craftsmanship in a way that generic purple never can.
